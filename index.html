Frequently Asked Questions

How is Milo different from other retail robots? +

Milo uses a unique Hybrid AI Architecture that combines computer vision with common sense reasoning. Unlike basic scanning robots, Milo understands context - it knows product sizes, typical store layouts, and can differentiate between actual out-of-stocks and misplaced items. This dramatically reduces false positives and provides truly actionable insights.

How does Milo navigate the store safely? +

Milo uses advanced LiDAR, computer vision, and proprietary navigation algorithms to safely navigate store environments. It detects and avoids obstacles, respects customer space, and operates at safe speeds. Our robots are designed to work alongside customers and staff without disruption.

What's the implementation process like? +

Implementation is designed to be seamless. We handle the entire setup process, including store mapping, system integration, and staff training. Most stores are operational within 2-4 weeks. Our team provides ongoing support to ensure smooth operation and maximum value.

What data does Milo collect and how is it used? +

Milo collects shelf-level data including product availability, placement, pricing, and promotional compliance. This data is processed securely and presented through our dashboard with actionable insights. We prioritize data security and privacy - your data belongs to you and is never shared without explicit permission.
